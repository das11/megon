Members only,
open to all.

The Friends Co-op is an inclusive community focused on sustainable creativity. As a member you gain access to resources, experimental projects, and a direct connection to unique collaborators.

Resources

Access tools, research, and space to get messy.

Progress happens faster when we build upon each other’s work, rather than repeat it.

Collective Knowledge

Search a library of research, helpful documents, assets, and design files.

Tools & Equipment

Checkout a range of equipment, from Magic Leap dev kits to power tools.

Events &
Studio Space

Members have access to studio space, team events, and outtings.

Projects

Speculate on preferred futures and alternative presents.

Members have access to experimental design projects. The first of which is Interstellar Sports—creating the first sport to be played in outer space and imagining what could happen here on Earth.

Community

Join a community of unique collaborators.

Design is at its best when diverse minds come together to solve complex challenges. Get curious with journalists, collab with filmmakers, ask for feedback from behaviorial economists, or just share your perspective—honesty encourages others to do the same.

Why a co-op?

Benefits for the modern workforce.

We started the co-op to share what we’ve learned and what we have with diverse collaborators that challenge the status quo. Risktaking should be rewarded and it’s in our nature to share because humanity, creativity, and purpose are at the core of what we do.

We work closely, we listen, we challenge, and we never stand still. We are Friends for a reason.
