Search Engine Optimisation

The process of ranking your website on the Google top search engine result pages. (SERP)

On-page Optimisation

The practice of optimizing your website’s individual webpages to rank higher in search engine result pages with relevant traffic to your business.

- Full website audit
- Meta tags analysis
- Google My Business integration
- Google search console integration
- Perfect keyword research
- Image alt tag optimization
- Google analytics integration

Off-page Optimisation

A technique that takes place outside your website. Off-page optimization helps you to rank well in Google search engine result pages. By building reliable backlinks to your business, we ensure that your website position is maintained on the first page of Google.

- Competitors backlinks analysis
- Business listings
- Social bookmarking
- Classified ads
- Guest posting
- Image submission
- Video submission
- Article submission
- Press release
- Directory submission
- Forum submission
